# 🏠 Real Estate — Arabic Content Domain

**Priority:** High — one of the highest-value Arabic content niches; Ramadan and year-end are peak
**Primary markets:** UAE (Dubai/Abu Dhabi off-plan dominant), KSA (Vision 2030 megaprojects), Egypt (New Cairo, New Administrative Capital), Qatar (post-FIFA development)
**Audience tiers:** First-time buyers, HNW investors, expat buyers, developers, real estate agents

## 2. Market-Specific Context

### Dubai / UAE
- **Off-plan dominance:** Most UAE real estate content is off-plan (pre-construction) — buying before a building exists
- **Expat-first audience:** 89% of Dubai residents are expats — content can be in English-Arabic mix; Arabic for Arab nationals and GCC investors
- **Key UAE selling points:** Golden Visa eligibility (for 2M AED+ purchases), rental yields (5-8% average in Dubai), freehold zones for foreigners
- **Mandatory disclosure:** "هذا الإعلان ترويجي. يرجى الرجوع إلى اتفاقية البيع والشراء للاطلاع على الشروط والأحكام الكاملة"
- **RERA compliance:** Dubai Real Estate Regulatory Agency requires registration and disclosure in ads
- **Nakheel / Emaar / DAMAC** are reference brand names that signal quality to Gulf buyers

### KSA
- **Vision 2030 megaprojects:** NEOM, The Line, Diriyah, Red Sea Project, ROSHN — these are the conversation context
- **Saudi national homeownership:** Government target to raise homeownership to 70%; REDF (Real Estate Development Fund) subsidies for Saudis
- **Gender in real estate:** Since 2019, Saudi women can independently own/purchase property — content can address women buyers directly
- **Islamic finance mandatory:** All mortgage/installment content must reference Sharia-compliant financing (مرابحة عقارية)
- **Wafa al-Arkan / ROSHN / SAMANA** are local developer reference names

### Egypt
- **New Administrative Capital (العاصمة الإدارية الجديدة):** Largest real estate story in Egyptian market history — premium apartments targeting upper-middle class
- **New Cairo / Sheikh Zayed / October:** Key suburban corridors for aspirational middle class
- **Price denomination:** Always in Egyptian pounds (EGP) with USD equivalent for cross-border buyers; note that prices shift rapidly with FX
- **Installment obsession:** Egyptian real estate is almost always sold on 7-15 year installment plans; "0% فوائد" is a major hook
- **Developer trust:** Egyptian buyers need to verify developer track record — mention completed projects, delivery history
- **Compound culture:** Gated communities with services (مجمع مسوّر بالخدمات) command 30-50% premium

### Qatar
- **Post-FIFA development:** Lusail City, Pearl Qatar, West Bay are premium areas
- **Non-Qatari ownership:** Restricted to specific zones (The Pearl, West Bay Lagoon) — always specify zone eligibility in content
- **HNW focus:** Qatar real estate content trends upmarket

## 3. Regulatory & Compliance Requirements

### UAE
- **Must include** broker/developer RERA registration number in ads
- **No guaranteed return claims** without substantiation: "عائد إيجاري متوقع" not "عائد إيجاري مضمون"
- **Off-plan disclosure:** Must state completion date and escrow account details
- **Map accuracy:** Published maps/renders must reflect reality — do not exaggerate proximity to amenities

### KSA
- **Real estate ads** must be registered with Ministry of Housing
- **Islamic finance:** All financing products must state Sharia compliance authority
- **No foreign ownership** claims unless property is in designated foreign ownership zones (specific NEOM/Vision 2030 zones)

### Egypt
- **Real Estate Developer Registration:** Developer must be registered with New Urban Communities Authority (NUCA) for New Cairo/capital projects
- **Cannot promise delivery date** without contractual guarantee
- **FX disclaimer:** Price quoted in EGP is subject to change with exchange rate for USD-pegged properties

## 4. Audience Tiers & Register

### First-time buyers (Egypt, KSA nationals)
- **Register:** L3 dialect, accessible
- **Tone:** Empowering, milestone-focused, family-centric
- **Hooks:** Affordability, installment ease, government support, dream home
- **Emotional core:** "أول بيتك" (your first home) is a powerful emotional anchor

### GCC Investors (UAE off-plan, international)
- **Register:** L4 or White Dialect
- **Tone:** ROI-focused, data-driven, international sensibility
- **Hooks:** Rental yield %, capital appreciation, visa eligibility, portfolio diversification
- **Platform:** LinkedIn, Instagram (premium visual), WhatsApp

### HNW / Luxury buyers
- **Register:** L4-L5, understated
- **Tone:** Exclusivity, craftsmanship, lifestyle
- **Never:** Price comparisons, urgency tactics, volume language
- **Platform:** Instagram (curated), email, private events, WhatsApp (concierge)

### Diaspora buyers (Egyptians abroad, Lebanese diaspora)
- **Register:** L3-L4 + diaspora emotion
- **Tone:** Nostalgia + investment logic + safety narrative
- **Hooks:** "استثمر في بلدك" (invest in your country), currency hedging, family roots
- **Platform:** Facebook (diaspora groups), Instagram, YouTube

## 7. Cultural Context

- **Home as legacy:** Owning property is deeply tied to Arab family legacy and identity — "مستقبل أولادك" (your children's future) is a genuine emotional hook, not manipulation
- **Family decision:** Major purchases are family decisions; content should acknowledge the family unit
- **View (إطلالة) is premium:** Sea view, park view, Mecca view — the view matters enormously and commands 20-40% premium; always feature in content
- **Compound culture in Egypt:** Gated communities signal safety and status; use "مجتمع راقٍ ومتكامل" framing
- **Taboo:** Never show alcohol, mixed-gender non-family settings in property lifestyle visuals for KSA/conservative Gulf — interiors and families are safe
- **Religious calendar:** Ramadan is peak inquiry time; people plan major purchases during Ramadan
