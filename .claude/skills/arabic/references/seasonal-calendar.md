# 🗓 Seasonal Calendar — Egyptian / Arab Campaign Timing

**Load when:** Campaign planning, `/arabic plan campaign`, or any time-sensitive brief.
**Pair with:** `references/ads-service-matrix.md`, `domains/ads-media.md`, `references/taboos.md` (religious-tone scan).

> **No fake recency.** This calendar is preloaded knowledge for *planning*, not a claim of live data. Never imply real-time trend access — say "حسب التقويم الموسمي", not "الترند دلوقتي". Lunar dates (Ramadan, Eids) **move ~11 days earlier each year** — always confirm the exact Gregorian date for the campaign year.

## 2. Ramadan — The Tentpole

- **Volume:** E-commerce surges 2×–4×; shopping peaks **11pm–3am** (post-iftar/suhoor).
- **Phases:**
  - *Pre-Ramadan (1–2 weeks before):* prep, pantry, "استعد لرمضان".
  - *First 10 days:* routine-building, recipes, family.
  - *Mid:* fatigue — value, convenience, time-saving.
  - *Last 10 days:* Eid gifting ramps; spiritual intensity (Laylat al-Qadr) — be respectful.
- **Tone:** generosity (الكرم), family (العيلة), gratitude (الامتنان), togetherness (لمّة).
- **Avoid:** exploiting religious sentiment for hard-sell, food imagery during fasting hours framed as temptation, anything frivolous around Laylat al-Qadr.
- **Masri hook sample:**
```
رمضان مش بس أكل وعزومات —
ده شهر اللمّة اللي بننتظرها السنة كلها 🌙
خلّي [البراند] جزء من سفرتكم.
```

---

## 3. Eids

### Eid al-Fitr (عيد الفطر)
- Joy, new clothes (الكسوة), kids' عيدية, gifts, family visits.
- Peak gifting + fashion + sweets (كحك العيد). Greeting: "كل سنة وانتم طيبين".

### Eid al-Adha (عيد الأضحى)
- Sacrifice (الأضحية), home, hosting, meat/freezer goods, travel to family.
- Tone: warmth, abundance, hosting. Handle sacrifice imagery tastefully.

## 6. Seasonal (non-holiday) Demand

- **Summer (Jun–Aug):** travel, fashion, cooling appliances, beach/Sahel (الساحل) culture in Egypt.
- **Back to School (late Aug–Sep):** stationery, uniforms, electronics, kids' content; parent-targeted.
- **Winter (Dec–Feb):** heaters, warm clothing, indoor entertainment.
- **Wedding seasons:** summer + post-Ramadan peaks — gifts, gold, venues, fashion.

---

## 7. Campaign Timing Rules

- **Lead time:** start Ramadan creative **2–3 weeks before**; awareness before, conversion during.
- **Confirm dates:** always verify the lunar date for the specific year before scheduling.
- **Don't stack tone-clashing offers:** somber/national dates ≠ hard discount pushes.
- **Night-weight Ramadan spend** toward the 11pm–3am window.
- **Localize the moment:** a White Friday push lands in KSA/UAE; in Egypt, lead with "الجمعة البيضا / الجمعة السوداء" per brand framing.
